1. I'm drunk, I love you.

The story of Carson's intense love for his closest friend Dio is told in I'm Drunk, I Love You. They go on a road trip before graduating from college and learn more about each other's feelings. As implied by the title, we witness the protagonists drinking throughout nearly the whole movie.
